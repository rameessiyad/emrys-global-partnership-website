Why is the Corporate mandatory for all Freezone and Mainland companies in UAE?

Corporate Tax is mandatory for all Free Zone and Mainland companies in the UAE due to the implementation of Federal Decree-Law No. 47 of 2022 on the Taxation of Corporations and Businesses.

All Free Zone Companies must register and file returns. May benefit from 0% on qualifying income, but 9% applies on non-qualifying income.

Mainland Companies: Subject to 9% Corporate Tax on taxable income exceeding AED 375,000.
